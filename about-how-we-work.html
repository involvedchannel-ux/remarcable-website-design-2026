About · How We Work

Three stages. Full transparency.

Our process has been refined over 150+ projects. It's built for clarity — so you always know what's happening, what's next, and what it costs.

01

Discovery

We begin every project by listening. A no-obligation consultation is followed by a site visit and practical evaluation. We're not pitching — we're learning. The output is a design brief that reflects exactly what you need.

  • Initial consultation to understand your goals, ambitions, and timeline
  • On-site survey and space assessment by our senior design team
  • Competitor and precedent analysis for your sector and location
  • Design brief and scope document issued for your sign-off
  • Ballpark budget guidance based on brief and site findings
02

Design

Your vision takes shape. We work through spatial planning, material selection, lighting, furniture, and bespoke joinery — iterating until every detail is right. Nothing progresses without your explicit approval.

  • Concept presentation: mood boards, spatial layouts, 3D visualisations
  • Material and finish schedule with sourced samples available to review
  • Furniture, lighting, and bespoke joinery specifications
  • Fixed-price proposal with phased payment schedule
  • Contractor selection and pre-construction coordination
03

Delivery

We manage the complete build. Our site teams coordinate contractors, suppliers, and all installations — minimising disruption and communicating progress every step of the way. Many clients trade throughout.

  • Full project management and contractor coordination
  • Weekly written progress updates and site access throughout
  • Phased delivery plans to maintain business continuity
  • Snagging inspection and sign-off before formal handover
  • 2-year workmanship warranty and aftercare package
Working With Us

What to expect

◎

A dedicated point of contact

You'll have one lead designer and one project manager throughout. No hand-offs, no confusion about who to call. The same people who hear your brief are the same people who deliver it.

◷

Weekly progress reporting

Every week during your build phase, you'll receive a structured progress report covering completed work, next steps, and any decisions needed from you. You're always informed, never chasing.

◈

No surprises on cost

Our fixed-price proposals cover everything agreed in scope. If something changes, we discuss it before it's instructed. You never receive an invoice for work you didn't expect or approve.
