Google Analytics

We may collect information about your computer, including your IP address, operating system, and browser type, for system administration and reporting. This is statistical data about browsing actions and patterns and does not identify an individual.

Google Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors engage with the website so we can improve performance and usability. These cookies can store information such as visit time, whether a visitor has been to the site before, and referral source.

Google Analytics reports trends anonymously. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking via your Google account settings or by contacting Google directly.

Google AdWords

This website may use Google AdWords remarketing services to advertise on third-party websites (including Google) to previous visitors — for example where a visitor did not complete a contact form.

Google Analytics conversion tracking may also be used to understand whether users who clicked ads completed actions on the website.

Cookies used in this process do not personally identify you, and you can opt out through Google settings at any time.
