The Founders
Portrait

Reni

Co-Founder & Creative Director

Reni leads creative direction across all Remarcable projects. With a background in fine art, environmental psychology, and extensive time spent studying interior and architectural design across Europe and Asia, she brings a rare fusion of instinct and evidence to every brief.

Her particular interest is in the emotional and psychological effects of the built environment — how spatial decisions shape the feelings and behaviours of the people inside. This conviction that design is not decorative but fundamentally therapeutic is the philosophical core of every Remarcable project.

"How do you want your space to make you feel? That's always the first question. Every decision that follows is in service of the answer."

When not on site or in the studio, Reni is most likely travelling — drawing inspiration from the built environments, natural landscapes, and cultural contexts that inform her material palette and spatial sensibility.

Portrait

Marc

Co-Founder & Project Director

Marc oversees the delivery, project management, and client relationships that bring Reni's creative vision to life on site. With a background in architecture and construction project management, he has spent over fifteen years learning how ambitious design is actually built — on time, to budget, and without compromising the vision.

His particular focus is the operational reality of each project: how to deliver a complete refurbishment while a dental practice continues to trade, how to phase a multi-floor office transformation without losing a single productive day, how to communicate clearly with clients who are trusting you with a significant investment.

"Design and guidance to help you make the right choices when scaling up. That's the promise — and the process is how we keep it."

Marc's systems, relationships, and instincts for delivery are the reason Remarcable has a 100% on-time, on-budget track record across 150+ projects — and why clients return for every new site.

Our History

Nine years of purposeful design

Remarcable was founded in 2016 by Reni and Marc, two design professionals who shared a conviction that the interior environments of businesses — clinics, offices, residences — were being treated as an afterthought when they should be a strategic priority.

Starting with a small network of healthcare clients in London, they built a reputation for clinical precision, genuine creativity, and a delivery model that never left clients guessing. Word spread quickly.

By 2020, Remarcable had expanded into commercial offices, residential developments, and care environments. The team grew — always selectively, always around craft and conviction — to include architects, artists, tradespeople, and wellbeing specialists.

Today, having delivered over 150 projects across the UK, Remarcable is recognised as one of the leading interior design studios for healthcare, commercial, and residential environments — a reputation built entirely on the work, and the people it serves.

How We Think

What makes us different

◎

Multidisciplinary by design

Our team spans interior architecture, environmental psychology, clinical compliance, construction management, and artisanal crafts. The breadth of expertise within a single studio is unusual — and it shows in the quality of the work.

◷

Inspired by travel and nature

Our design thinking is shaped by the built environments we encounter in our travels and the balance we find in the natural world. Every project brings something back from that exploration — a material, a proportion, a quality of light.

◈

Selective and committed

We take on a defined number of projects per quarter. Not because we can't handle more, but because we believe in giving every client our full attention. Our relationships are long-term — many clients have worked with us across five or more sites.
