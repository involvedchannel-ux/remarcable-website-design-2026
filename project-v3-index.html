The Brief

The established clinic sought additional space to enhance safety and comfort for both patients and staff. Key focus areas included the reception desk, seating arrangements, staff corner, and merchandise layout. The primary goal was to improve the space without disrupting ongoing operations, while enabling future scaling of the practice.

Reception Seating Staff Areas Future Scaling
02 / 03

Our Concept

We integrated existing building features to minimise unused space and optimise workflow. Key considerations centred on decluttering and visual minimalism. Sustainability was emphasised through natural materials and eco-certified textured wallpaper. Colour psychology principles were applied based on space purpose, natural light conditions, and the desired emotional impact on patients and staff.

Minimalism Sustainability Colour Psychology Workflow
03 / 03

Our Thinking

We created a rejuvenated and purpose-driven space that blends functionality with minimalism. Curated light colours and soft tones inspired by the client's existing brand palette were selected to evoke a tranquil atmosphere — one that supports the therapeutic nature of the clinic while projecting quiet confidence to new patients arriving for the first time.
