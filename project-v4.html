The Brief

The established clinic sought additional space to enhance safety and comfort for both patients and staff — improving reception, seating, staff corner, and merchandise layout without operational disruption.

ReceptionSeatingStaff Areas
02 / 03

Our Concept

We integrated existing building features to minimise unused space. Sustainability was emphasised through natural materials and eco-certified wallpaper. Colour psychology applied based on space purpose and natural light.

MinimalismSustainabilityColour Psychology
03 / 03

Our Thinking

Curated light colours and soft tones inspired by the client's brand palette evoke a tranquil atmosphere — functionality and minimalism working in quiet counterpoint.

Bespoke Reception Desk

The focal design piece features a higher upstand for privacy and space delineation, with enhanced storage capabilities. A clear separation between client and staff areas reduces visual clutter throughout the reception zone.

Seating Area
Highlight 02

Seating Installation

Comfortable bench seating with soft cushions and backrests, designed around decluttering, privacy, safety, and support — ensuring patients feel at ease from the moment they arrive.

Wallpaper

Eco-Certified Texture

Hand-crafted design featuring coloured petals — cornflowers, roses, sunflowers, moss — scattered on a white base. Carbon-neutral production in Tyrol. Suitable for large-area applications throughout the clinic.

STANDARD 100 OEKO TEX  ·  White recycled cellulose & textile fibres
See highlighted area on image
Colour Palette

Tonal Calm

Soft tones drawn from the client's brand palette, selected using colour psychology principles. Each room's specific natural light conditions and therapeutic purpose informed the final palette decisions.

Colour psychology  ·  Wellness-focused  ·  Brand-aligned
See highlighted area on image
Joinery

Bespoke Cabinetry

Custom reception desk and storage built to exacting staff workflow requirements. The higher upstand creates privacy delineation while integrated storage eliminates desk clutter, maintaining the visual calm of the space.

FSC certified timber  ·  Custom fabrication  ·  Staff-workflow driven
See highlighted area on image
Lighting

Layered Warmth

Ambient, task, and accent layers combining to reinforce the clinic's calming atmosphere while meeting medical-grade luminance requirements. Ceiling fixtures were chosen to complement the wallpaper's organic language.

CIBSE healthcare guidelines  ·  LED throughout  ·  3000K warm white
