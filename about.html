Our Why

Remarcable was born from a simple conviction: that the spaces we inhabit every day should actively support the people inside them. Reni & Marc founded the studio after witnessing how poorly-designed environments held businesses back — and how the right design could transform them.

Our purpose is to inspire positive change in work environments, one thoughtfully designed space at a time.

How We Work

Our process is built around clarity, not creativity for its own sake. Every project follows three defined stages — Discovery, Design, and Delivery — with full cost transparency and client sign-off at each gate.

We've developed our approach over 150+ projects, refining it each time. The result is a model that works for clients whether they're refurbishing a single room or transforming multiple floors.

4 Key Elements

Every Remarcable project is built on four evidence-based design pillars: light & atmosphere, nature & plants, bespoke furniture & branding, and colours & mood. These aren't decorative flourishes — they're the tools we use to create spaces that perform.

Understanding why these elements matter is the first step toward a space that truly works.
