Our Process

Three stages.
Zero surprises.

A straightforward model built around your timeline, your budget, and your vision.

01 Discovery
02 Design
03 Delivery
01

Discovery

We begin by understanding your world — your clients, your team, your ambitions. A no-obligation consultation is followed by a site visit to evaluate your space, constraints, and opportunity.

→Initial consultation — understanding your goals, timeline, and budget envelope
→On-site survey and space assessment by our senior design team
→Competitor and precedent analysis for your sector and location
→Design brief and scope document issued for your approval
02

Design

Your concept takes shape. We work through spatial planning, materials, furniture, lighting, and branding — iterating until every detail is exactly right. Nothing progresses without your sign-off.

→Concept presentation: mood boards, spatial layouts, and 3D visualisations
→Material and finish schedule with sourced samples
→Furniture, lighting, and bespoke joinery specifications
→Fixed-price proposal with phased payment schedule
03

Delivery

We project-manage the complete build, coordinating contractors, suppliers, and installations. Our site teams work to minimise disruption — many projects are delivered while clients continue to trade.

→Full project management and contractor coordination
→Weekly progress updates and site visit access throughout
→Snagging, handover, and aftercare package
→2-year workmanship warranty on all completed work
