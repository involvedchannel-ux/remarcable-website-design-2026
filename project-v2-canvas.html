Project Explanation — Scroll to read
01 / 03  ·  The Brief

A clinic ready for the next chapter

The established clinic sought additional space to enhance safety and comfort for patients and staff — improving the reception desk, seating, staff corner, and merchandise layout without disrupting ongoing operations.

02 / 03  ·  Our Concept

Sustainability woven into every decision

We integrated building features to minimise wasted space and optimise workflow. Sustainability was emphasised through natural materials and eco-certified textured wallpaper — colour psychology applied to each room based on natural light and desired emotional impact.

03 / 03  ·  Our Thinking

Tranquility through restraint

Curated soft tones inspired by the client's brand palette evoke a tranquil atmosphere. Functionality and minimalism work in counterpoint — the space feels purposeful, calm, and quietly confident.

Section Highlights
Reception Desk
Highlight 01

Bespoke Reception Desk

The focal design piece features a higher upstand for privacy and space delineation, with enhanced storage capabilities. The upstand creates a clear separation between client and staff areas while reducing visual clutter throughout the reception zone.

Seating Area
Highlight 02

Seating Installation

Black and white artwork complemented comfortable bench seating with soft cushions, backrests, and open-bottom design. Design priorities: decluttering, privacy, safety, and support — ensuring patients feel at ease from the moment they arrive.

Second Floor
Highlight 03

Second Floor Integration

When the client acquired the upper floor, design elements were seamlessly integrated to ensure continuity in both the aesthetic and atmosphere — extending the same spatial language across both levels of the clinic.
