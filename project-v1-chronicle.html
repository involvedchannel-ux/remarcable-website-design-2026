01

The Brief

+

The established clinic sought additional space to enhance safety and comfort for both patients and staff. Key focus areas included the reception desk, seating arrangements, staff corner, and merchandise layout. The primary goal was to improve the space without disrupting ongoing operations, while enabling future scaling of the practice.

Read full brief →
02

Our Concept

+

We integrated existing building features to minimise unused space and optimise workflow. Key considerations centred on decluttering and visual minimalism. Sustainability was emphasised through natural materials and eco-certified textured wallpaper. Colour psychology principles were applied based on space purpose, natural light conditions, and the desired emotional impact on patients and staff.

Read full concept →
03

Our Thinking

+

We created a rejuvenated and purpose-driven space that blends functionality with minimalism. Curated light colours and soft tones inspired by the client's existing brand palette were selected to evoke a tranquil atmosphere — one that supports the therapeutic nature of the clinic while projecting quiet confidence to new patients.

Section Highlights
Reception Desk Image
Highlight 01

Bespoke Reception Desk

The focal design piece features a higher upstand for privacy and space delineation with enhanced storage capabilities. Driven by staff requirements, the upstand creates a clear separation between client and staff areas while reducing visual clutter throughout the reception zone.

Seating Image
Highlight 02

Seating Installation

Black and white artwork complemented comfortable bench seating with soft cushions, backrests, and open-bottom design. Design priorities included decluttering, privacy, safety, and support — ensuring patients feel at ease from the moment they arrive.

Second Floor Image
Highlight 03

Second Floor Integration

When the client acquired the upper floor, design elements were seamlessly integrated to ensure continuity in both the aesthetic and atmosphere — extending the same spatial language across both levels of the clinic.
