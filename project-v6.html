The Brief

The established clinic sought additional space to enhance safety and comfort for both patients and staff — improving reception, seating, staff corner, and merchandise layout without operational disruption.

ReceptionSeatingStaff Areas
02 / 03

Our Concept

We integrated existing building features to minimise unused space. Sustainability was emphasised through natural materials and eco-certified wallpaper. Colour psychology applied based on space purpose and natural light.

MinimalismSustainabilityColour Psychology
03 / 03

Our Thinking

Curated light colours and soft tones inspired by the client's brand palette evoke a tranquil atmosphere — functionality and minimalism working in quiet counterpoint.

Section Highlights
Reception Desk Image
Highlight 01

Bespoke Reception Desk

The focal design piece features a higher upstand for privacy and space delineation with enhanced storage capabilities. The upstand creates a clear separation between client and staff areas while reducing visual clutter throughout the reception zone.

Seating Area Image
Highlight 02

Seating Installation

Black and white artwork complemented comfortable bench seating with soft cushions, backrests, and open-bottom design. Design priorities: decluttering, privacy, safety, and support — ensuring patients feel at ease from the moment they arrive.

Second Floor Image
Highlight 03

Second Floor Integration

When the client acquired the upper floor, design elements were seamlessly integrated to ensure continuity in both the aesthetic and atmosphere — extending the same spatial language across both clinic levels.
