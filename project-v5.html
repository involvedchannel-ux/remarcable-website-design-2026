The Brief

The established clinic sought additional space to enhance safety and comfort for both patients and staff — improving reception, seating, staff corner, and merchandise layout without operational disruption.

ReceptionSeatingStaff Areas
02 / 03

Our Concept

We integrated existing building features to minimise unused space. Sustainability was emphasised through natural materials and eco-certified wallpaper. Colour psychology applied based on space purpose and natural light.

MinimalismSustainabilityColour Psychology
03 / 03

Our Thinking

Curated light colours and soft tones inspired by the client's brand palette evoke a tranquil atmosphere — functionality and minimalism working in quiet counterpoint.

TranquilityBrand PalettePatient Experience

Bespoke Reception Desk

The focal design piece features a higher upstand for privacy and space delineation, with enhanced storage capabilities. A clear separation between client and staff areas reduces visual clutter throughout the reception zone.

Seating Installation

Comfortable bench seating with soft cushions and backrests, designed around decluttering, privacy, safety, and support — ensuring patients feel at ease from the moment they arrive.
